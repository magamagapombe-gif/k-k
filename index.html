The Process

How Kevin & Klein Works

Six simple steps from your request to your doorstep. No risk, no fraud, no wasted transport money.

📝1

Fill the Form

Tell us what you want, where to find it, and where to deliver — one step at a time.

💬2

We Confirm

We contact you on WhatsApp to confirm your order details and agree on payment.

💳3

You Pay

Send the product cost + our 10% service fee via MTN or Airtel Mobile Money. Fully secure.

🏪4

We Shop

Our team physically visits the shop, verifies product quality, and buys exactly what you ordered.

📸5

We Verify

We send you photos of the actual product before dispatching. What you see is what you get.

🚚6

We Deliver

Your package is dispatched via trusted transport. WhatsApp updates until it reaches you.

Simple, Transparent Pricing

We charge a flat 10% on your total purchase amount. If you're ordering UGX 100,000 worth of items, our fee is just UGX 10,000 — far less than what you'd spend traveling to Kampala yourself. Minimum order: UGX 10,000.

Why Choose Us

We Solve the Real Problems

  • 🛡️

    Protection from Online Fraud

    Fake online sellers are a serious problem in Uganda. We physically go to the location — you never pay a fraudster again.

  • 💰

    Save on Transport Costs

    A round trip Mbale–Kampala costs UGX 60,000. Our fee on a 100K order is just 10K. The math works every time.

  • 📸

    Photo Proof Before Dispatch

    We send you clear photos of the actual product before it leaves Kampala. What you see is what you get.

  • 📱

    WhatsApp All the Way

    Every update — from confirmation to delivery — comes to your WhatsApp. No apps to install, no accounts to create.

  • 🏪

    We Reach Any Shop You Specify

    Give us a TikTok link, a Facebook page, a phone number, or a shop name. We go there and buy on your behalf.
