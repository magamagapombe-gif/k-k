The Process

How Kevin & Klein Works

Six simple steps from your request to your doorstep. No risk, no fraud, no wasted transport money.

📝1

Fill the Form

Tell us what you want, where to find it, and where to deliver. Include your WhatsApp number.

💬2

We Confirm

We contact you on WhatsApp to confirm your order details, quote the full cost, and agree on payment.

💳3

You Pay

Send the product cost + our 10% service fee via MTN or Airtel Mobile Money. Fully secure.

🏪4

We Shop

Our team physically visits the shop, verifies the product quality, and purchases exactly what you ordered.

📸5

We Verify

We send you photos of the actual product before dispatching, so you see what you're getting.

🚚6

We Deliver

Your package is dispatched via trusted transport. You get tracking updates until it reaches you.

Simple, Transparent Pricing

We charge a flat 10% on your total purchase amount. That's it. No hidden charges, no surprises. If you're ordering UGX 100,000 worth of items, our fee is just UGX 10,000 — far less than what you'd spend traveling to Kampala yourself. Minimum order is UGX 10,000.
